About Ejis Cargo

Ejis Cargo is a leading vehicle and shipping company specializing in selling trucks & cars and shipping goods and vehicles from the UK to Nigeria using secure 20ft and 40ft containers.

Our Mission

To make vehicle procurement and cross-border shipping reliable, transparent, and stress-free for businesses and individuals across Nigeria.

Why customers choose us

  • Rigorous vehicle inspection & documentation
  • Competitive pricing and transparent fees
  • Secure container loading & experienced logistics partners
  • End-to-end support: customs clearance, inland delivery, tracking
